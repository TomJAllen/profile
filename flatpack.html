Flatpack Features

Flatpack Features started as a lockdown project to improve our flat. As we began researching how to make some common home improvements and replicated them for multiple rooms, an idea blossomed about how this could be offered as a product.

We then drafted out the idea, undertook some market research and put together a simple landing page to gauge interest.

While projects took precidence, the algorythms created to define materials required for any room
